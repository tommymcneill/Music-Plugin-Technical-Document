Introduction

Solo Piano is a cross-platform, virtual instrument created in mind for music producers who need an on-the-go small and lightweight piano plugin that is intended to sound great placed over the top of busy mixes. It has one purpose, to sound great over the top of bands and busy recordings. Most VST plugins and piano sounds on the market struggle to cut through a mix without some extra processing.


This is why Solo Piano was created. Out of the box, it is designed to sound great over busy mixes. This instrument was created by Tommy Solo, a keyboard player who has experimented for years collecting and playing keyboards live, experimenting and hand picking the best keyboard sounds in order to craft piano solos over the top of songs.


After trialling thousands of presets and searching through many keyboards and pianos, the creators found that it is very rare for a piano sound to cut through a crowded and busy song. Most keyboard presets sound great for the purpose of a piano only recording, however in a full track are underwhelming, Many presets are designed to sound great in a classical recording, but when you add the crashing drums and a heavy wall of guitar sound, it is hard for many sounds to really do the job.


Therefore, a specific piano has been expertly selected to be sampled in order to sit perfectly over mixes for this virtual instrument.


Inside a DAW environment (for example, Logic, Ableton, Pro Tools), you can access this virtual piano to construct piano lines over your songs.

Program Features

  • An industry leading designed sound on the market for those looking for a preset that cuts through the mix when doing Piano Solos
  • Reverb knobs to allow for manipluation and shaping of amazing piano sounds.
  • A special slideable "solo" knob which focuses the piano sound from being a wide, rhythmic sound suitable for accompanyment, to an upfront "piano solo" sound, that places the piano in the middle of the mix.
  • Server-side support for installation

How To Install

This guide assumes you have the following Logic, Ableton, Pro Tools, FL Studio

  • Download and run the Installer
  • Select to Install the VST preset for users of FL Studio, Ableton
  • Select to Install the .Component for users of Logic Pro
  • Select to Install the .aax preset for users of Pro Tools
  • Restart your System

Solo Feature

The Solo feauture is a knob that effectively widens or narrows the piano sound. For a good solid, knocking piano solo, you may wish to make use of this knob. Ideally, for a piano part that is playing accompaniment, where the piano needs to sit with the drums, as more of a background element, you would want the Solo knob to be more towards the left.
For a piano solo where the focus of the song moves to the piano, where you would want the piano recording to peak over the drums and have a strong focus and clarity to the sound, you would want to switch the "Solo" knob all the way to the right. This should put your piano at the center of the mix and give it predominance.

Reverb Knobs

To get started with the reverb knobs, open the instrument and begin experimenting. The reverbs are taken from one of the creators secret boards, a very popular industry keyboard from the 2000s. Apply this knob and make your piano solo float even more over the top of the mix.

reverb knob left = off
reverb knob right = on

Enjoy making use of the reverb!
